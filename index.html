Travis Fischer

Tech Consultant. Nerd.

Travis Fischer saw Star Wars when he was six and wanted his own R2-D2, which sparked a life-long love of technology and innovation. Travis has spent the last ten years working across the aerospace, oil & gas, financial, and mobile application sectors to make techology more accessible and understandable in our everyday lives.

Travis is currently Director of Engineering at ChaiOne, a Houston-based digital consultancy in the oil and gas, power, and industrial verticals. He’s a Virtual Reality tool builder, data science dabbler, and walkability and sustainability advocate.
